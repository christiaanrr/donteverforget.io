Welcome!

dontforget.io is dedicated to serve as your personal to-do list application. As a college student, I was fed up with all the deadlines and projects I had to do. Often, I would forget what I had to because of all the assignments that were being given to me.

No worries, dontforget.io will solve all your problems. You can easily add the courses you are taking and add a todo list item for that course. As long as you put a due date for that todo list item, dontforget.io will send you a text three hours prior to your todo item being due!
